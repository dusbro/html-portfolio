The Best Movies according to Dustin

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

The Big Lebowski

Just an absolute classic of a comedy. Jeff Bridges is 100% now and forever the dude!

Se7en (Seven)

One of my all time favorite thrillers. The first time I watched this I was on the edge of my seat. Could not believe what was in the box in the end.

My Neighbor Totoro

This was the very first anime I had ever watched when I took Japanese in 6th Grade. Studio Ghibli has never failed to deliver masterpieces.
